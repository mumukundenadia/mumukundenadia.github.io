Jeremy Bentham The philosopher and jurist Jeremy Bentham (1748-1832) were born at Houndsditch, London, on 15th February 1748.he proved to be something of a child prodigy: while still A toddler he was discover sitting at his father’s desk reading a multi-volume history of England, and he began to study Latin at the age of three. At twelve, he was sent to Queen’s college oxford, his father, a prosperous attorney, having decided that Jeremy would follow him into the law, and feeling quit sure that his brilliant son would one day be Lord chancellor of England
